Our Story

Where Ideas
Come Alive

TEDxVBIT is an independently organized TED event held at Vignana Bharathi Institute of Technology, Hyderabad. We bring together the most curious minds like engineers, artists, scientists and dreamers to ignite conversations that matter.

Every year, we challenge comfortable thinking, celebrate radical honesty, and create a space where the impossible becomes a starting point.

IDEAS
2026 Theme

SOLVE
FOR Y

Life rarely gives us all the variables, yet asks us to find meaning anyway. This theme explores curiosity, ambiguity, and the courage to seek answers.

01

QUESTIONS & UNCERTAINTY

Sitting with questions that don't have immediate answers, and learning to move forward even when things don't fully make sense.

02

PATTERNS & PERSPECTIVES

Connecting the dots between experiences, and seeing how different viewpoints shape the way we understand our own path.

03

CHOICE & COURAGE

Making decisions without complete clarity, and trusting yourself to figure things out along the way.

Everything You
Need to Know

TEDxVBIT is an independently organized TED event hosted at Vignana Bharathi Institute of Technology, Hyderabad. Under the TED license, we curate speakers and performers to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group.

Anyone with a curious mind — students, professionals, entrepreneurs, artists, scientists. If you believe ideas have the power to change the world, TEDxVBIT is your gathering place.

Click on the register now CTA and complete the registration form. You'll receive an email for payment. Once it is done, you will receive your digital pass with event details.

Select talks will be professionally filmed and published on the TEDx YouTube channel after the event. However, attending in person is an experience that no recording can replicate.
